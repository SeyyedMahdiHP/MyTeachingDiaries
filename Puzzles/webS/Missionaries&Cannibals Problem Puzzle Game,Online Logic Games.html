Online Puzzle Game – Cannibals & Missionaries

Rating: 6.7/10 - 4812 votes

Missionaries & Cannibals: Help the 3 cannibals and 3 missionaries to move to the other side of the lake. Note that when there are more cannibals on one side of the lake than missionaries, the cannibals eat them. So you need to think logically and strategically in order to get them all across to the other side safely. Can you solve the puzzling problem? Have fun!

How to Play: Use your computer mouse to click on a cannibal to put him on the boat and also click on a missionary to put him on the boat. In order to move them to the other side of the lake, click the ‘GO’ button at the top of the game screen.
